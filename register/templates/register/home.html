Please click the tab on the right side of the page for the region where the brevet that you want to pre-register for is.

These pages provides a pre-registration service for brevets that simply lets the ride organizer know that you are intending to ride. We collect your name and email address so that we can confirm your pre-registration to you and the organizer by email.

We also provide a link to the event waiver form that you can print, read carefully, complete and sign, and bring to the start with you to save time and brain-power in the early dawn hours. If you're not already a member of the club there is also a link to the club membership form that you can also print, read carefully, complete and sign, and bring with you.

This is not a credit card payment site, so you'll still need to bring your $15 (cash or cheque) with you to the start to pay for the event (and another $20 if you'll be paying for membership at the start too).

Questions about the brevets should be directed to the ride organizers. Feedback about this site can be sent to {{ admin_email }}

If you are a brevet organizer and would like set up pre-registration for your ride, please read the Info for Brevet Organizers page.
